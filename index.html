About Me

Hi, I’m Janina | a frontend developer-in-the-making with a background in healthcare and a degree in Network and IT-Security. Originally from Germany, I’ve called Norway home since the mid-2000s.

My interests live somewhere between logic and creativity. I love crafting responsive, accessible websites with a strong sense of design and storytelling. Whether it’s creative coding, subtle animations, or making sure everything feels just right, I enjoy the details that turn code into experience.

Outside of dev life, I’m into books, design, art, games, and anything that blends imagination with technology. I value time with family and friends just as much as diving into a good solo coding session or learning something new.

I’m passionate, detail-oriented, and happiest when I get to build something meaningful | whether it’s with a team or on my own. I’ll be graduating as a frontend developer in about a year, and I’m excited for the projects ahead.
